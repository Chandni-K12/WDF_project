Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is an e-library?

An e-library is a digital platform offering access to books, academic journals, articles, multimedia, and other educational resources online.

Who can use the e-library?

Registered users including students, faculty, researchers, and the general public (depending on access level) can use the e-library.

Is the e-library free to use?

Basic access may be free, but some resources might require institutional login or paid subscription based on the library's policies.

What types of resources are available?

The e-library offers e-books, audiobooks, research journals, magazines, videos, databases, and other educational content.

Account & Login

How do I register for the e-library?

Click on the 'Sign Up' button on the homepage, then enter your email, name, and create a password to register.

I forgot my password — how can I reset it?

Click 'Forgot Password' on the login page. A reset link will be sent to your registered email address.

Can I use my school/university login to access the e-library?

Yes, if your institution is partnered with the e-library. Use your institutional credentials on the login page.

Can I change my email or profile details?

Yes, go to your profile settings and update your email or other personal information.

Using the E-Library

How do I search for books or journals?

Use the search bar to enter keywords, titles, or author names. Filters help refine results by category or date.

How can I read a book online?

Click on the book title and select 'Read Online'. A viewer will open with options to navigate pages and adjust text settings.

Can I download books for offline reading?

Yes, many resources are downloadable in PDF or ePub formats, depending on the content license.

Is there a mobile app for the e-library?

Yes, you can download our app from Google Play Store or Apple App Store for mobile access.

Can I take notes or highlight in e-books?

Yes, our platform supports highlighting text and adding notes. These features are saved to your account for future reference.

Borrowing & Access Rules

Is there a borrowing limit?

Yes, users can borrow up to 5 books at a time. Borrowing limits may vary based on user type.

How long can I keep an e-book?

E-books can typically be accessed for 14 days. After that, access will expire unless renewed.

Can I renew borrowed books?< p>Yes, you can renew e-books before the due date if there are no restrictions or waitlists.
What happens if I exceed my borrowing time?

The e-book will be automatically returned and access revoked. You can borrow it again if it’s available.

Technical Support

Why isn't my book loading?

Check your internet connection and try refreshing the page. If it still doesn’t work, clear your browser cache or try another browser.

Which browsers/devices are supported?

The e-library works on all modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) and devices including laptops, tablets, and smartphones.

I’m having trouble opening a PDF/ePub file. What should I do?

Ensure you have a compatible reader app installed. Try downloading the file again or contact support for assistance.

Content & Collection

How often is new content added?

New content is added weekly or monthly, depending on publisher agreements and user demand.

How do you select books or journals for the library?

Selections are made based on academic relevance, user feedback, licensing availability, and partnerships with publishers.

Can I request a specific book to be added?

Yes, you can request new books via the 'Suggest a Book' option or through email. We review all suggestions regularly.

Do you offer content in multiple languages?

Yes, we offer multilingual content including books and journals in English, Hindi, and other regional/international languages.

Privacy & Security

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, your data is encrypted and stored securely. We follow industry-standard security practices to protect user data.

Will my reading history be tracked?

Reading history may be tracked to improve recommendations but is not shared with third parties without consent.

Do you share user data with third parties?

No, we do not share or sell personal data to third parties. Data is only used for library operations and improvements.

Feedback & Support

How can I contact support?

You can reach us via the 'Contact Us' page or email us at support@elibrary.com. Support is available 24/7.

How can I give feedback or suggestions?

Visit the 'Feedback' page and submit your suggestions or comments. We value your input!

Where can I find user guides or tutorials?

User guides and tutorials are available under the 'Help' section of the website.
